How to throw a ball:
  1. Stand up straight.
  2. Turn sideways 90 degrees to your right (If right handed).
  3. Feet shoulder-width apart.
  4. Have left arm pointing at target
  5. Lift right hand so ball is near the ear.
  6. In one motion: Shift weight to front foot, drop pointing left arm,
    twist torso, bring right arm over your shoulder, and release ball

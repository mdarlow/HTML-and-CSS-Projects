    Fish Pictures & Facts

    Aquarium keeping can reduce stress and create a powerful sense of fulfillment by watching a tiny underwater world grow before your eyes and knowing that you were the one who made it happen. For live fish to thrive under your ownership, however, you must understand the basics of fish-keeping and the needs of individual aquatic species. Said lack of understanding will lead to a poor living environment for your underwater pets, which will inevitably lead to disease and very possibly death.
